I research the sociology and construction of information technology. I am a scientist specialist at the Dutch Organisation for Applied Scientific Research (TNO) as part of the unit on ICT, Strategy, and Policy.

I earned my PhD in information systems management from the University of Luxembourg (Thesis: Politics, Power, and Information Systems). My research focused on three technologies – blockchain, identity systems, and the metaverse – which I approach in a critical manner.

Having successfully collaborated and published with lawyers, mathematicians, and computer scientists, my strength lies in cross-disciplinary collaboration, to find logical relations between topics, and to untangle socio-technological controversies. Amongst other outlets, I contributed impactful research featured in:

  • Technology in Society
  • Philosophy & Technology
  • Government Information Quarterly
  • Computer Law and Security Review
